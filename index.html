The Problem We Solve

Why Digital Skills Matter in Bangladesh

Employers increasingly require basic digital literacy, but thousands of young adults in Jessore lack access to training.

💼

Job Market Gap

Over 70% of job listings now require basic digital skills like email, spreadsheets, and online communication — skills many young adults in Jessore never had the chance to learn.

🇧🇩

Taught in Bangla

Every workshop is conducted in Bangla, removing language as a barrier. No confusing English interfaces — we make digital literacy truly accessible to everyone.

📱

Smartphone First

Many participants don't have a laptop, but they have a smartphone. Our workshops are designed so you can practice everything directly on your Android phone.

🎓

Practical, Not Theoretical

Every session ends with a real deliverable: a sent email, a completed CV, a submitted job application. We measure success by what you can do, not what you learned.

🤝

Community-Led

This project is run by a fellow young adults who understand the challenges firsthand. Proximity to the problem drives every design decision.

🌱

UN SDG Aligned

DigiWork directly advances SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) by equipping marginalized youth with tools to participate in the digital economy.
