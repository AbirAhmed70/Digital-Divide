FAQ

Common Questions

Are the workshops really free?

Yes, 100% free. No registration fee, no materials fee. DigiWork is a community initiative funded through the Millennium Fellowship program.

Do I need a laptop or computer?

No! All workshops are designed to work on smartphones. If you have an Android phone, you have everything you need.

Are the workshops in Bangla?

Yes. All sessions are conducted in Bangla so that language is never a barrier to learning.

Who can attend?

Any young adult aged 18–35 from Jessore or surrounding areas who is looking to improve their digital skills for employment.

Can I attend all 8 workshops?

Absolutely! We encourage you to register for all workshops. Each one builds on the previous, but they also work as standalone sessions.
